# ScambuchiaCheck

ScambuchiaCheck la tien ich mo rong cho Chrome/Edge giup nguoi dung kiem tra thong tin minh bach cua cac trang Facebook truoc khi nhan tin, mua hang hoac giao dich.

Tien ich se doc noi dung Page Transparency hien thi tren Facebook va canh bao cuc bo neu phat hien dau hieu nguoi quan ly trang co lien quan den Campuchia. Neu thong tin quan ly trang lien quan den Viet Nam, tien ich co the hien thi trang thai an toan hon.

## Tinh nang

- Tu dong kiem tra trang Facebook khi nguoi dung truy cap.
- Ho tro URL trang Facebook thong thuong va dang `profile.php?id=...`.
- Doc noi dung Page Transparency truc tiep trong trinh duyet.
- Thu doc trang transparency lien quan neu nguoi dung dang o trang chinh cua page.
- Hien canh bao rui ro neu phat hien thong tin quan ly trang lien quan den Campuchia.
- Tu dong xoa canh bao khi nguoi dung chuyen sang trang Facebook khac.
- Xu ly cuc bo tren trinh duyet, khong gui du lieu nguoi dung len may chu ben ngoai.

## Quyen rieng tu

Tien ich nay khong thu thap, luu tru, ban, truyen tai hoac chia se du lieu ca nhan cua nguoi dung.

Tat ca qua trinh kiem tra duoc thuc hien cuc bo trong trinh duyet. Tien ich chi doc noi dung hien thi tren Facebook va noi dung transparency lien quan de hien thi canh bao cho nguoi dung.

## Cai dat de test

1. Mo Chrome hoac Edge.
2. Truy cap `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Bat `Developer mode`.
4. Bam `Load unpacked`.
5. Chon thu muc `fb-cambodia-scam-detector`.

## Ghi chu khi dang Chrome Web Store

Tien ich can quyen truy cap `facebook.com` de doc noi dung Page Transparency hien thi tren Facebook va hien canh bao truc tiep trong trinh duyet.

Ly do xin quyen de dien tren Chrome Web Store:

```text
Required to read visible Facebook Page Transparency content on facebook.com and display local browser warnings. No user data is collected or transmitted.
```

## Luu y

Tien ich chi dua ra canh bao rui ro mang tinh tham khao. Tien ich khong dua ra ket luan phap ly va khong khang dinh chac chan rang mot trang la lua dao.

Nguoi dung nen kiem tra them cac dau hieu khac nhu lich su hoat dong cua trang, danh gia, thong tin lien he, phuong thuc thanh toan va phan hoi tu cong dong truoc khi tuong tac hoac giao dich.
